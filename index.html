Popular Puzzle Types

3x3 Cube: The classic Rubik's Cube. Known for its challenging gameplay and numerous solving techniques.

2x2 Cube: Also known as the Pocket Cube. It's smaller and simpler than the 3x3 cube, making it a great starting point for beginners.

Pyraminx: A pyramid-shaped puzzle. It's intuitive and easy to solve, making it popular among beginners and enthusiasts alike.
